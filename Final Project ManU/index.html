Welcome to Manchester United

We were founded in Manchester in 1878 and known as Newton Heath L&YR Football Club. In 1902 we were offically reborn as Manchester United.

Sir Alex Ferguson
Sir Alex brought this club success the world has never seen before. From the 1991/1992 until 2013 when he retired Sir Alex never finsihed below 3rd in the league.
History continues to be written
Manchester United first gained promotion into the top division in 1892. From there they have become arguably the biggest football club in the world. All across the globe you will find Manchester United jerseys being worn. Club legends such as George Best, Bobby Charlton, Ryan Giggs, Sir Alex Ferguson, Wayne Rooney, Christiano Ronaldo and countless others have always brought the spotlight on this club. We have experienced the lowest of lows with the Munich Air Disaster and the highest of highs such as the treble in 1999. Today our biggest challenge is the owners. We the fans continue to fight for a chance to rid this club of the current regime and return our club to its rightful place as the best club in the world.
